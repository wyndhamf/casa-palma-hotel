Nuestra Historia

A Warm Welcome
in Playa del Carmen

Casa Palma Hotel is more than a place to stay — it's your home in the heart of Mexico's Riviera Maya. Nestled on the vibrant Avenida Juárez, we offer the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and authentic Mexican hospitality.

Whether you're here for the turquoise waters, the world-famous nightlife, or simply to unwind under the Caribbean sun, Casa Palma puts you right where the magic happens — just minutes from the beach, 5th Avenue, and the Cozumel ferry.

Our family-run hotel provides clean, comfortable rooms with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and 24-hour front desk service, all at a price that lets you spend more on experiences and less on accommodation.
